How to delete your Tuskee account 

Deleting your account is easy; simply follow the steps below

You need to have access to the Tuskee app and your login details to delete your account. If you have forgotten your login details, you can simply reset your password on the login page of the app using the email address you used to create the account. 

Open your Tuskee app

If you are logged out, use your login details to access the app and your account


Open the menu drawer 

At the top-left side of the home screen, tap the menu button (hamburger button) to access the menu options

Go to "settings and privacy"

This is where you have control over your privacy settings and other Tuskee preferences 


Select "delete account"

You will see a red button on the screen; tap the button to delete the account 



Confirm account deletion

A pop-out will appear asking you to confirm the deletion of your account, and all of your profile details (i.e., username, name, bio, login details) will be deleted 

Important: how to delete all of your Tuskee data

It may take longer for some of your data (i.e., posts, messages, images, files) to be deleted; this is what you need to do to delete this data

You can delete your data (i.e., messages, posts, images, and files) manually via the app; however, this will not be possible if you have deleted your account. 

Based on the applicable laws of your country, you may have the right to request access to the personal information we collect from you, change that information, or delete it.

To request to review, update, or delete your personal information and data, please fill out and submit a data subject access request form. The process of deleting your data may take up to 90 days.
